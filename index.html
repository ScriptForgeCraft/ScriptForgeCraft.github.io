About Company Mobile App

DIGNISI is a specialized company engaged in primary real estate market sales. It was founded in 2020 by experienced real estate professionals.

The company collaborates with 130 development firms, representing 80% of Armenia’s real estate market, and offers more than 170 projects both in Armenia and abroad.

“Dignisi” translates from Latin as “dignified.” The company was founded on extensive experience, which is reflected in its rapid expansion.

From the very beginning, the company’s mission has been to work with dignity and to create a transparent environment.

Recently, DIGNISI has also started cooperating with several Armenian banks, allowing buyers to check their creditworthiness directly at our office, saving their most valuable resource: time.

One of our company’s key advantages is the DIGNISI application. It functions like a real estate expo, providing extensive opportunities for both developers and buyers.

It is clear that the primary goal of any developer is to increase sales, and our app offers all the essential tools needed to support and enhance the sales process.

For buyers, the app provides access to over 80% of Armenia’s new developments in one unified platform, making the decision-making process significantly easier.

The application also gives developers advertising opportunities — an excellent way to expand their reach even further.
